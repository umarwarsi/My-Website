Returns & Exchanges

Our hassle-free return policy:

  • 7-day return window from delivery date
  • Item must be unused with original packaging
  • Perfumes and personal care items are final sale
  • Return shipping is at customer's expense (unless item is defective)

To initiate a return, contact our customer service with your order number and reason for return. We'll guide you through the process.

Exchange process:

  1. Contact us within 7 days of receiving your order
  2. Return the original item in unused condition with all packaging
  3. Once received, we'll ship the replacement item

Notes:

  • Customer pays return shipping
  • Exchanges subject to product availability
  • Price differences must be settled before exchange

We're sorry if your item arrived damaged. Here's what to do:

  1. Take photos of the damaged item and packaging
  2. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  3. We'll arrange for return shipping at our expense
  4. Receive replacement or full refund once verified

For COD orders, please refuse delivery if package appears damaged.

Product Care

To maintain your URBANGENTS leather wallet:

  • Daily Care: Wipe with soft, dry cloth
  • Monthly: Apply small amount of leather conditioner
  • Avoid: Water, direct sunlight, excessive heat
  • Storage: Keep in dust bag when not in use
  • Usage: Don't overstuff - maintains shape

Our full-grain leather develops a beautiful patina over time with proper care.

Maximize your fragrance's longevity:

  • Application: Spray on pulse points (wrists, neck, behind ears)
  • Preparation: Apply after showering to moisturized skin
    • Technique: Don't rub - let it dry naturally
  • Layering: Use matching shower gel/lotion if available
  • Storage: Keep in cool, dark place away from sunlight

Our Eau de Parfum concentration typically lasts 6-8 hours on skin.

Care instructions for URBANGENTS watches:

  • Cleaning: Wipe with soft, dry cloth regularly
  • Water Exposure: Check water resistance rating before swimming
  • Chemicals: Avoid contact with perfumes, lotions, cleaning agents
  • Storage: Keep in provided case when not worn
  • Service: Have battery replaced by professional when needed

For leather straps, avoid water and condition occasionally.
