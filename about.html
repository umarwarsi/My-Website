Explore Our Journey

Our Humble Beginnings

From Lahore to Your Wardrobe

Founded in 2023 in the heart of Lahore, URBANGENTS was born from a simple vision: to provide Pakistani men with high-quality, stylish accessories that combine international standards with local sensibilities. What began as a small workshop crafting premium leather wallets has grown into a complete men's fashion brand offering watches, perfumes, belts, and more.

Our founder, a Lahore native with a passion for men's fashion, noticed a gap in the market for locally available, premium men's accessories that didn't compromise on quality or style. URBANGENTS was created to fill that void, bringing together the finest materials and craftsmanship to elevate the modern Pakistani gentleman's style.

Our Journey

Milestones in the URBANGENTS story

Mid 2024 - Watch Collection

Late 2024 - Retail Expansion

2025 - What's Next
